Privacy Policy

TAP STACK TOWER — SHANKS STUDIO
Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Shanks Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Tap Stack Tower ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Tap Stack Tower does not directly collect any personal information from users. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data and interaction data
  • Device information (OS version, device model)

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: google.com/settings/ads

3. Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your high score and best score
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Daily challenge progress

This data is stored using Unity PlayerPrefs and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

5. Advertising

The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. These ads may be personalized based on your interests. You may see a consent dialog when first launching the App in certain regions (e.g., EU/EEA) in compliance with GDPR.

6. Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting any information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (EU users)
