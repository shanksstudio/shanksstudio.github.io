Privacy Policy

SHANKS STUDIO
🗼 Tap Stack Tower: Build Blocks 🎨 Color Road Runner 🧩 Nonogram Master: Picture Cross ⚔️ Spin Fury: Blade Survivor
Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Shanks Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our mobile games Tap Stack Tower: Build Blocks, Color Road Runner, Nonogram Master: Picture Cross, and Spin Fury: Blade Survivor (collectively referred to as "the Apps"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Our Apps do not directly collect any personal information from users. However, the Apps use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Neither App requires account registration or collects names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

2. Third-Party Services

Both Apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data and interaction data
  • Device information (OS version, device model)

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: google.com/settings/ads

3. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers:

Tap Stack Tower: Build Blocks

  • Your high score and best score
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Daily challenge progress

Color Road Runner

  • Personal best distance / score
  • Unlocked color skins and cosmetics
  • Game settings and preferences

Nonogram Master: Picture Cross

  • Completed levels and star ratings (1–3 stars per level)
  • Overall progress (puzzles solved out of 200)
  • Remove Ads purchase status
  • Audio mute preferences

Spin Fury: Blade Survivor

  • Highest wave / stage reached
  • Unlocked characters and weapon builds
  • Remove Ads purchase status
  • Audio mute preferences

All locally stored data is handled via browser localStorage / Unity PlayerPrefs and remains entirely on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through one of our Apps, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

5. Advertising

Both Apps display ads provided by Google AdMob, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. These ads may be personalized based on your interests. Users in certain regions (e.g., EU/EEA) will see a consent dialog on first launch in compliance with GDPR and the EU User Consent Policy.

6. Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting any information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (EU users)
