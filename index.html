Our Story

Born to Sail,
Built to Conquer

Shanks Studio is a solo independent mobile game studio forging hyper-casual and action experiences for players across the globe. Where others see limitations, we see open ocean.

Inspired by the greatest pirates who ever lived — the ones who didn't need a map, who sailed without permission — we approach game development the same way: bold, fearless, and relentless.

"It's not about following a map. It's about having the courage to sail without one."
— The Shanks Studio Creed
